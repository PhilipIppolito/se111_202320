About The Island


The Island is a Sci-Fi action thriller that takes place in the year 2019. A time when cloning yourself has become normal for the rich. It is believed that your clone is always in a vegetative state and will never gain consciousness, but that has been a lie the whole time. After Lincoln Six Echo discovers a bug in the facility, he becomes suspicious of what he was told his whole life. He and the thousands of other clones living underground in a secret facility are unaware of their creation. They have been told there was a contamination and they must live in the facility. There is hope, though, where they hold daily lotteries to be freed to the island, the only place on earth that has not been contaminated. After Lincoln discovers the truth, he tries to escape the facility with Jorden Two Delta, who had just won the lottery the night before and was preparing to depart to the island. With the help of one of the facility staff, Lincoln has become friendly with the two heads to LA to find Tom Lincoln, Lincoln's sponsor. After some intense escaping from the police and the search team sets out to find the two escapee products; they can shut down the facility, and everyone inside the facility can enter into the real un-contaminated world.
